How to Promote
a Recruiting
Website in the USA

Traffic Growth of Around 200% — Huntly Case Study

Project Duration: October 2023 – June 2024 Target Region: USA Service: SEO

Who is Our Partner

Huntly.ai is a platform that connects IT recruiters with international product companies. It specializes in recruiting tech talent for U.S. and European firms. Huntly's mission is to revolutionize recruitment and simplify the hiring process for all parties involved. This startup is rapidly growing in both
the American and European markets.

Promotion Goals

Huntly's target audience consists of employers seeking candidates for their companies and posting job openings on Huntly. Thus,
our tasks were as follows:

  1. Optimize the website to meet the needs of the target audience, attracting the first employers in the USA.
  2. Increase visibility and organic traffic to boost income
    from successful job placements.

Team Actions

The recruitment niche is saturated with competitors,
so we first thoroughly analyzed the competition and website structure.
At the start of the project, Huntly.ai had only two main categories.
We recommended expanding the structure to meet the high standards
of the competitive environment. The partner quickly implemented
our recommendations, allowing us to successfully execute
changes and achieve results.

Frequent questions about split-testing

  • Page Title Optimization

    Analyzing and optimizing page titles to meet SEO best practices.

  • H1 Tags

    A common mistake is that the main heading is marked as H2 or missing, but it should be H1 for better understanding by search engine bots.

  • Alt Tag Recommendations

    Proper image description in the code for indexing.

  • Open Graph

    For better presentation of the site on social media platforms.

  • Usability Audit

    Identifying and fixing issues with user interaction on the site.

  • robots.txt Setup

    Ensuring proper site indexing.

  • 301 Redirects

    Redirecting from old URLs to new ones.

  • Breadcrumbs Implementation

    Improving site navigation.

  • XML Sitemap Creation

    Facilitating site indexing by search engines.

  • Broken Link Removal

    Improving site quality and indexing.

  • Speed Optimization

    Optimizing images, minimizing CSS and JavaScript files, and using caching.

Huntly's team quickly implemented the technical requirements, making the website more
competitive and attractive to search engines.

Content Marketing: Expanding the Site Structure and Creating Quality Content

Content creation and publishing were crucial elements of our strategy. We focused on expanding the site structure to meet the needs of the target audience — employers. Currently, the "For employer" category has a two-level nesting structure.

After analyzing user queries, we gathered a semantic core and created technical tasks for content creation, which the Huntly team executed. This resulted in high-quality and useful content for site visitors.

An important part was establishing a blog. We knew the recruiting niche required constant knowledge updates and experience sharing. Huntly focused on articles that address real problems specialists face in their work.

Topics include the most effective talent search strategies, how to conduct technical interviews, and the latest trends in IT recruiting. Each article is researched and written with up-to-date data and practical advice. After implementation,
the blog presents the most relevant topics

Building Partnerships

Collaborating with news sites and authoritative sources provided quality backlinks. This helped create a foundational backlink profile, which we continued to build. This is a key factor for high search rankings. Huntly's team averaged 8-9 placements per month. Initially, articles on external platforms linked to two main categories and the homepage. After expanding the huntly.ai structure, new link acceptors were added, significantly expanding the backlink profile

Conclusion

Thanks to detailed niche analysis, a systematic approach to text metadata preparation, and testing of graphical elements, the team achieved significant results in the selected countries, expanding the app's user base. After the ASO work, the app’s visibility nearly doubled, and installs grew by a third. Creative testing showed conversion improvements of 2-3% with each iteration.
